* Office 365
** Outlook mail setup
   1. Click gear-shaped icon (right top side?)
   2. Click "View all Outlook settings"
   3. Click "Mail -> Forwarding"
   4. You can remove forwarded mails with the option "Keep a copy of forwarded messages"
** Check capacity
   1. Click gear-shaped icon (right top side?)
   2. Click "General -> Storage"
